## First, Get This Straight: Leads Aren't Just Names—They're Engaged Folks Ready to Buy

<p>
Hormozi hits hard: A lead isn't some random email; it's someone you can contact who's shown interest in what you sell. Engaged leads? They're the ones opting in because you dangled something juicy—a "lead magnet" that solves a real pain fast.
</p>

<p>
For SMBs, this flips the script from spray-and-pray marketing. Running a coffee roastery? Don't blast generic ads; offer a free "5-Minute Brew Guide" PDF that hooks home baristas. Suddenly, you're not chasing; they're coming to you.
</p>

<p>
We apply this at Prism by crafting lead magnets baked into your designs—like pop-up opt-ins on sites or app demos that capture emails. Our content team whips up ebooks or videos tailored to your niche, feeding straight into funnels that nurture 'em to sales. Result? Warmer leads, higher close rates, less wasted ad spend.
</p>

## Build Multiple Pipes: Don't Put All Eggs in One Lead Basket

<p>
The book breaks lead gen into four buckets: Warm outreach (folks you know), free content (build goodwill), cold outreach (DMs, emails), and paid ads (scale fast). Key? Diversify so if one dries up, you're not screwed.
</p>

<p>
Startup teams, listen up—this is your anti-fragile playbook. A SaaS founder might warm-reach old colleagues, post killer LinkedIn threads, cold-email prospects from conferences, and run targeted Facebook ads. Mix 'em, and leads flow steady.
</p>

<p>
Prism amps this with tech: We design multi-channel funnels—AI-powered chatbots for warm chats, content calendars for organic reach, automated cold sequences via apps, and ad creatives that A/B test on autopilot. For a local bakery client, we blended Instagram Reels (free content) with Google Ads, tripling walk-ins in a month.
</p>

## Master the Content Game: Hook 'Em, Keep 'Em, Reward 'Em

<p>
Hormozi's Hook-Retain-Reward system is fire for free content. Hook with a bold promise, retain by delivering value bombs, reward with calls-to-action that lead to your offer. Post everywhere: Social, blogs, podcasts—build that "free goodwill" Hormozi raves about.
</p>

<p>
For SMBs like consultants or e-com brands, this means ditching boring posts for stuff that educates and entertains. A fitness coach? Hook with "Ditch Diets Forever," retain with workout tips, reward with a free trial link.
</p>

<p>
We live this at Prism: Our designs make content pop—eye-catching sites, video embeds, SEO-juiced blogs. Add AI for personalization (like suggesting related reads), and your retain game levels up. We built a content funnel for a tech startup that turned weekly posts into 50+ monthly leads, no ads needed.
</p>

## Scale Smart: Turn Customers into Lead Machines

<p>
Once leads roll in, get "lead getters"—referrals from happy clients, affiliates who promote for commissions, even agencies or employees hustling for you. Hormozi calls this leveraging others to multiply your reach without more work.
</p>

<p>
In the SMB trenches, this is gold for bootstrappers. A web dev agency? Reward clients with discounts for referrals. Affiliates? Partner with bloggers in your space.
</p>

<p>
Prism weaves this in via tech: Referral widgets on apps, affiliate dashboards in custom software, automated email campaigns thanking buyers and asking for intros. For one client, a referral program we designed via their site generated 30% of new leads, cutting acquisition costs in half.
</p>
