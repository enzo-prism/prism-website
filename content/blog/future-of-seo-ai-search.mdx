<h2 class="text-2xl font-bold mt-10 mb-6">The Big Shift: From Keywords to Concepts</h2>
<p class="mb-6">
  In the past, SEO was a game of keywords. You wanted to rank for "best coffee shop in San Francisco." AI Search thinks differently. It doesn't just match words; it understands <em>ideas</em>. It wants to find the most authoritative, clear, and helpful source on a given <em>concept</em>.
</p>
<p class="mb-6">
  Your new goal isn't just to rank for a keyword-it's to <strong>"own" a concept.</strong> You want AI to recognize your website as the definitive resource for your niche.
</p>

<hr class="my-8" />
<h2 class="text-2xl font-bold mt-10 mb-6">Your LLM SEO Action Plan & Checklist</h2>
<p class="mb-6">Here's how to start making tangible changes today. Go through this checklist for your own business.</p>

<h3 class="text-xl font-semibold mt-6 mb-4">✅ Step&nbsp;1: Define and Own Your Core Concept</h3>
<p class="mb-4">You can't be everything to everyone. AI rewards clarity and focus. You need to decide what single, powerful idea you want to be known for.</p>
<p class="mb-4">
  <strong>Action:</strong> As a team, answer this question: "If an AI could recommend only one company for [your service/product], why should it be us?" The answer is the foundation of your core concept. It might be "sustainable custom home building" or "user-centric mobile app design."
</p>
<ul class="list-disc pl-6 mb-6 space-y-1">
  <li>[ ] We have clearly defined the primary concept we want to "own" (e.g., "AI-powered logistics for small businesses").</li>
  <li>[ ] Our homepage and key service pages communicate this core concept clearly and repeatedly.</li>
</ul>

<h3 class="text-xl font-semibold mt-6 mb-4">✅ Step&nbsp;2: Structure Your Content for a Robot Reader</h3>
<p class="mb-4">AI models "crawl" your site to learn. If your site is messy or confusing, they'll give up and go elsewhere. You need to make your content incredibly easy for a machine to read and categorize.</p>
<p class="mb-4">
  <strong>Action:</strong> Organize your website content with a logical hierarchy. Use clear headings, short paragraphs, and bullet points to break up complex topics. Think of it as creating a perfect outline for a research paper.
</p>
<ul class="list-disc pl-6 mb-6 space-y-1">
  <li>[ ] Our website pages use proper heading structure (a single H1 for the title, followed by H2s for main sections, and H3s for sub-points).</li>
  <li>[ ] Our content is scannable, using bullet points and numbered lists where appropriate.</li>
  <li>[ ] The technical foundation of our site is clean (this is where we at Prism come in, ensuring things like sitemaps and structured data are pristine).</li>
</ul>

<h3 class="text-xl font-semibold mt-6 mb-4">✅ Step&nbsp;3: Prioritize Depth and Originality</h3>
<p class="mb-4">AI is designed to filter out shallow, repetitive content. It's looking for deep, original insights that genuinely help the user. A single, comprehensive article is now more valuable than ten short, fluffy blog posts.</p>
<p class="mb-4">
  <strong>Action:</strong> Create "pillar content"-a definitive, in-depth guide on your core concept. Think of the most common questions your customers ask and answer them all in one place with your unique perspective.
</p>
<ul class="list-disc pl-6 mb-6 space-y-1">
  <li>[ ] We have at least one piece of pillar content that comprehensively covers our core concept.</li>
  <li>[ ] Our content provides original insights and a clear point of view, not just rehashed information.</li>
</ul>

<h3 class="text-xl font-semibold mt-6 mb-4">✅ Step&nbsp;4: Keep it Fresh and Build Real Authority</h3>
<p class="mb-4">An old, dusty website signals to AI that your information might be out of date. Likewise, AI is learning to distinguish between real, authentic citations and spammy links.</p>
<p class="mb-4">
  <strong>Action:</strong> Set a calendar reminder to review and update your most important content at least twice a year. Seek out genuine collaborations, guest appearances on podcasts, and mentions in industry publications that link back to your site.
</p>
<ul class="list-disc pl-6 mb-6 space-y-1">
  <li>[ ] We have a schedule (e.g., quarterly or bi-annually) to review and refresh our key content.</li>
  <li>[ ] We are actively working to earn authentic citations and links from other reputable sources.</li>
</ul>
