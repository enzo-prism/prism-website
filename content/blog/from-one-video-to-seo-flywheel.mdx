<h2 class="text-2xl font-bold mt-10 mb-6">What You'll See</h2>

<p class="mb-6">
  In the video below, I take a single YouTube recording and transform it into:
</p>

<ul class="list-disc pl-6 mb-6 space-y-2">
  <li>A polished blog post optimized for search</li>
  <li>Social media clips for Twitter and LinkedIn</li>
  <li>A live website page deployed instantly</li>
</ul>

<p class="mb-6">
  The best part? This entire process is repeatable. You can do this with any video content you create.
</p>

<h2 class="text-2xl font-bold mt-10 mb-6">The Tools I Used</h2>

<p class="mb-6">
  Here's my exact tech stack for this content transformation:
</p>

<ul class="list-disc pl-6 mb-6 space-y-2">
  <li><strong>Cursor AI</strong> - For intelligent code editing and content drafting</li>
  <li><strong>Gemini 2.5 Pro</strong> - To optimize content for AI search</li>
  <li><strong>GitHub + Vercel</strong> - For instant deployment and preview</li>
  <li><strong>YouTube</strong> - As the source for transcripts and video</li>
</ul>

<h2 class="text-2xl font-bold mt-10 mb-6">The Simple Process</h2>

<h3 class="text-xl font-semibold mt-6 mb-4">Step 1: Extract Your Content</h3>
<p class="mb-6">
  Start with your YouTube video. Export the auto-generated transcript-this becomes your raw material. Don't worry about it being perfect; we'll clean it up.
</p>

<h3 class="text-xl font-semibold mt-6 mb-4">Step 2: Transform with AI</h3>
<p class="mb-6">
  Paste the transcript into Cursor AI. Ask it to create a structured blog post with clear headings and bullet points. The AI turns spoken words into readable prose in seconds.
</p>

<h3 class="text-xl font-semibold mt-6 mb-4">Step 3: Optimize for Search</h3>
<p class="mb-6">
  Take your draft to Gemini. Ask it to optimize for AI search engines and add FAQ schema. This future-proofs your content for how people search today.
</p>

<h3 class="text-xl font-semibold mt-6 mb-4">Step 4: Deploy Instantly</h3>
<p class="mb-6">
  Commit your markdown file to GitHub. Vercel automatically deploys it. You can preview, fix any issues, and go live-all within minutes.
</p>

<h3 class="text-xl font-semibold mt-6 mb-4">Step 5: Create Social Clips</h3>
<p class="mb-6">
  Use AI to identify the best moments from your video. Extract 30-60 second clips for social media. Each clip links back to your full content.
</p>

<h2 class="text-2xl font-bold mt-10 mb-6">Why This Works</h2>

<p class="mb-6">
  This approach solves three major problems:
</p>

<ul class="list-disc pl-6 mb-6 space-y-2">
  <li><strong>Time:</strong> What used to take hours now takes minutes</li>
  <li><strong>Consistency:</strong> You maintain a regular publishing schedule</li>
  <li><strong>Reach:</strong> One piece of content serves multiple channels</li>
</ul>
